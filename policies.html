Financing

DHOP operates at a profit. We use this profit to reinvest into our business to make aspects of the business more effiecient. An example of this is our overnight delivery system. We are currently funding research into getting a delivery system for the weekend as well.

Delivery

Our overnight delivery happens during the business week. Items ordered over the weekend will arrive on the following Monday.

Return Policies

Our store has a 30 day return policy. If the purchase is unopened, it may be returned after 30 days for a full refund.
